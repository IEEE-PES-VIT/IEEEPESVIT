About PES
WHO WE ARE
IEEE Power and Energy Society is a chapter that helps in providing latest technological developments in the power and energy industry and helps in informing individuals on how to use the power and energy resources judicially and aims and educates for a sustainable, resource-friendly, green and an eco-friendly future. This chapter not only helps students to boost their skills but also, helps to conserve the environment.
OUR VISION
Our vision is to work with motivated young individuals to inspire others to work for a better future by educating them to conserve power and energy resources/capital and working on projects on building skills for the same. Our vision is to make an impact in the power and energy society , help in preservation of the various ecosystems and work on new and challenging ideas.
ABOUT VIT
Vellore Institute of Technology provides excellent educational opportunities, world-class facilities and remarkable mentors. This highly esteemed university provides us a platform to cultivate new ideas and a space for critical thinking through our chapter
